This directory contains badge icons for the rewards system.

The following badges are used in the app:
- pomodoro_novice.png - Complete 5 pomodoro cycles
- pomodoro_master.png - Complete 25 pomodoro cycles
- pomodoro_expert.png - Complete 100 pomodoro cycles
- task_starter.png - Complete 10 tasks
- task_achiever.png - Complete 50 tasks
- goal_setter.png - Complete 5 daily study goals
- goal_crusher.png - Complete 20 daily study goals
- study_beginner.png - Accumulate 5 hours of study time
- study_pro.png - Accumulate 25 hours of study time
- study_champion.png - Accumulate 100 hours of study time
- consistent_bronze.png - Maintain a 3-day study streak
- consistent_silver.png - Maintain a 7-day study streak
- consistent_gold.png - Maintain a 14-day study streak
- consistent_diamond.png - Maintain a 30-day study streak

Note: If badge images are not found, the app will display a default icon as a fallback.
